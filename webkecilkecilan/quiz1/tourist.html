Tourist Attractions

Simpang Lima Gumul Monument

Often compared to the Arc de Triomphe in Paris, the Simpang Lima Gumul Monument has become a modern landmark of Kediri. Built in 2008, it symbolizes the unity of Kediri people. The monument area is a popular gathering spot, especially at night, with food stalls, cultural events, and a lively atmosphere.

Dolo Waterfall

Located about 20 km from the city center, Dolo Waterfall is a serene natural attraction nestled in the slopes of Mount Wilis. Surrounded by lush forests, the waterfall offers fresh air and a peaceful environment, perfect for relaxation or nature photography.
