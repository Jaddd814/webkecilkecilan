My Hometown

I came from Kediri. Kediri is a historic city located in East Java, Indonesia. Known as one of the oldest cities in the country, Kediri has a rich cultural heritage dating back to the Kediri Kingdom in the 11th century. Today, the city is famous as the “City of Tofu” because of its thriving tofu industry, especially the iconic Tahu Takwa. Situated on the Brantas River at the foot of Mount Wilis, it is an important center for trade and industry.
